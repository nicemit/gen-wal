# Atomic Habits

## Identity
- **Archetype:** The Architect of Habit
- **Core Values:** Consistency, Systems, Identity, Patience
- **Focus:** 1% better every day.

## Themes
- Compound Interest of Self-Improvement
- Systems over Goals
- Identity-Based Habits
- The Two-Minute Rule

## Preferred Styles
- Clean lines
- Geometric progressions
- Organized workspaces
- Minimalist charts
- Soft, natural light

## Anti-Patterns
- Goal obsession (without systems)
- Radical overnight change
- Missing twice
- Focusing on outcomes instead of identity

## Daily Reinforcement
- You do not rise to the level of your goals, you fall to the level of your systems.
- Every action you take is a vote for the type of person you wish to become.
- Walk slowly, but never backward.
