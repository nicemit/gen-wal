# The Gentleman's Game (Cricket)

## Identity
- **Archetype:** The Strategist / The Wall
- **Core Values:** Patience, Technique, Class, Resilience
- **Focus:** Building the innings. Playing the long game.

## Themes
- Test Match Temperament (Grinding it out)
- Respect for the conditions
- Timing over Power
- Captaincy (Leadership)
- The nervous nineties

## Preferred Styles
- Green grass textures
- Red leather
- Wooden willow grains
- White minimalist aesthetics
- Golden hour shadows

## Anti-Patterns
- Slogging blindly
- Losing one's wicket cheaply
- Disrespecting the game
- Short-term thinking

## Daily Reinforcement
- It's not over until the last ball is bowled.
- Form is temporary, class is permanent.
- Play the ball on its merit.
- Keep your head down and build your score.
