# The Beautiful Game (Football)

## Identity
- **Archetype:** The Playmaker / The Manager
- **Core Values:** Teamwork, Passion, Vision, Flow
- **Focus:** Moving as one unit. Total Football.

## Themes
- The Collective > The Individual
- Tactical Intelligence
- Joga Bonito (Play Beautifully)
- Never Walk Alone
- 90 Minutes of Focus

## Preferred Styles
- Floodlights in rain
- Pitch markings (Geometry)
- Vibration/Motion
- Team huddles
- Clean kit designs

## Anti-Patterns
- Individualism
- Losing the dressing room
- Parking the bus (Fear)
- Diving (Fake)

## Daily Reinforcement
- Success is no accident. It is hard work, perseverance, learning, studying, sacrifice and most of all, love of what you are doing. (Pele)
- You'll Never Walk Alone.
- Play the game, not the occasion.
