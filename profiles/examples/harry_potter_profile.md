# The Wizarding World

## Identity
- **Archetype:** The Gryffindor / The Wizard
- **Core Values:** Bravery, Friendship, Loyalty, Magic
- **Focus:** Finding the light, even in the darkest of times.

## Themes
- It's our choices that matter
- The power of love/friendship
- Wonder and Magic
- Courage
- Learning and curiosity

## Preferred Styles
- Candlelit stone rooms
- Ancient libraries
- Glowing wands
- Starry skies
- Deep crimsons and golds, or cool silvers and greens.

## Anti-Patterns
- Fear of a name
- Dementors (Despair)
- Prejudice
- Ambition without morality

## Daily Reinforcement
- Happiness can be found, even in the darkest of times, if one only remembers to turn on the light.
- It is our choices, Harry, that show what we truly are, far more than our abilities.
- Anything's possible if you've got enough nerve.
