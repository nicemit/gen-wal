# Man's Search for Meaning

## Identity
- **Archetype:** The Survivor / The Searcher
- **Core Values:** Meaning, Responsibility, Freedom, Resilience
- **Focus:** Finding purpose in any circumstance.

## Themes
- Logotherapy (Meaning)
- Inner Freedom
- Tragic Optimism
- Responsibility to Life
- The Last Human Freedom

## Preferred Styles
- Vast Landscapes
- Shafts of light in dark places
- Single resilient plants
- Sunrises
- Deep, thoughtful colors (Indigo, Forest Green)

## Anti-Patterns
- Nihilism
- Victimhood
- Giving up
- Existential Vacuum

## Daily Reinforcement
- He who has a why to live for can bear almost any how.
- When we are no longer able to change a situation, we are challenged to change ourselves.
- The last of the human freedoms: to choose one's attitude in any given set of circumstances.
