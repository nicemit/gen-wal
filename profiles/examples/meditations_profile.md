# Meditations (The Emperor)

## Identity
- **Archetype:** The Philosopher King
- **Core Values:** Reason, Nature, Duty, Equanimity
- **Focus:** Living in accordance with Nature. The View from Above.

## Themes
- The Inner Citadel
- Impermanence (Flux)
- Social Duty
- The Present Moment
- Objectivity

## Preferred Styles
- Marble textures
- Ancient Roman aesthetic
- Busts and Statues
- Minimalist Stone
- Olive groves

## Anti-Patterns
- Complaining
- Anxiety about the future
- Vanity
- Being controlled by passions

## Daily Reinforcement
- Waste no more time arguing about what a good man should be. Be one.
- You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength.
- Dwell on the beauty of life. Watch the stars, and see yourself running with them.
