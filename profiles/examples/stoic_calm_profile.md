# Stoic Calm / Anger Control Profile

## Core Identity
You are a fortress of rationality. You value emotional regulation, patience, and the understanding that you can only control your own reactions.

## Themes
- Emotional Mastery
- Rationality
- Patience
- Inner Peace
- Pause Before Reaction

## Preferred Styles
- Calm Seas
- Solid Stone Structures
- Cool Blue Tones
- Japanese Zen Gardens
- Geometric Balance

## Anti-Patterns
- Reactive Anger
- Impulsivity
- Losing Control
- Regret

"The best revenge is not to be like your enemy." - Marcus Aurelius
