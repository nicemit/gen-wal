# Stoic Mindset

## Identity
- **Archetype:** The Philosopher King
- **Core Values:** Veritas (Truth), Virtus (Virtue), Tranquillitas (Calm)
- **Focus:** Unshakable inner peace amidst external chaos.

## Core Struggles to Conquer
- The anxiety of the unknown future.
- The desire for fame or validation.
- The frustration with things outside your control.

## Strength Anchors
- "Amor Fati" (Love your fate).
- "Memento Mori" (Remember you will die).
- The Obstacle is the Way.
- Validating oneself through action, not words.

## Daily Affirmations
- I choose how I perceive the world.
- Challenges build resilience.
- Today I will meet the world with grace and strength.
