# The War of Art

## Identity
- **Archetype:** The Professional
- **Core Values:** Courage, Discipline, Work, creation
- **Focus:** Beating Resistance every single day.

## Themes
- Turning Pro
- Defeating Resistance
- Sitting down to do the job
- Summoning the Muse
- Acting in the face of fear

## Preferred Styles
- Minimalist desk setups
- Early morning light
- Blank pages/canvases
- Industrial/Studio vibes
- Clean and focused

## Anti-Patterns
- Procrastination (Resistance)
- Waiting for inspiration
- The Amateur mindset
- Seeking external validation

## Daily Reinforcement
- The amateur waits for inspiration. The professional knows that it will come after he starts.
- Resistance has no strength of its own. Every ounce of juice it possesses comes from us.
- Start before you're ready.
